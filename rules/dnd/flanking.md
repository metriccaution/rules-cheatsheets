---
title: Flanking (Optional Rule)
reference: Dungeon Master's Guide 251
---

- **Optional Rule**
- Two creatures are flanking an enemy when they are on directly opposite sides of it
  - Both must be able to attack the enemy (in reach, not incapacitated, etc.)
  - Both must be on the ground (not mounted, flying, etc.)
- Both flanking creatures have **advantage** on melee attack rolls against that enemy
- Flanking only works with melee attacks
- **Large** or larger creatures only need one of their spaces to qualify for flanking
- A creature with reach greater than 5ft can flank from beyond 5ft if its reach allows
