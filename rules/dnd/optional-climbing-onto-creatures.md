---
title: Climbing Onto a Bigger Creature (Optional Rule)
reference: Dungeon Master's Guide 271
---

- **Optional Rule**
- Use instead of a standard grapple when a smaller creature climbs onto a larger one
- Climber makes a **Strength (Athletics)** or **Dexterity (Acrobatics)** check
  - Contested by the target's Dexterity (Acrobatics) check
- If successful, the climber
  - Moves into the target's space
    - Can move around on the target, as **difficult terrain**
  - Moves _with_ the target
  - The climber has **advantage** on attack rolls against the target
- The target
  - The target can still attack the climber, at the DM's discretion
  - The target can knock the climber off with a contested Strength (Athletics) vs the target's Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics)
