---
title: Passive Checks
reference: Player's Handbook, page 175
---

- A **passive check** is a fixed score used instead of a die roll
- Score = 10 + all modifiers that normally apply to the check
  - Add +5 if the creature has **advantage** on the check
  - Add −5 if the creature has **disadvantage** on the check
- Used when a task is routine, repeated, or when the DM wants to check without tipping off players
- Most common use: **passive Wisdom (Perception)** to notice hidden creatures or things
  - e.g. +3 Perception modifier → passive Perception of 13
