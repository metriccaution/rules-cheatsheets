# Stats

Fairly self-explanatory:

- **Strength**
- **Speed**
- **Intellect**
- **Combat**

# Saves

- **Sanity**
  - Making sense out of the insensible, chaos
  - Coping with stress
- **Fear**
  - Dealing with emotional effects and consequences
- **Body**
  - Physical effects, damage to your own person
