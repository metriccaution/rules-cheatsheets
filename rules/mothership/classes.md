# Classes

- Marine
  - Soldiers and fighters — handy in a fight, but their panic is contagious and dangerous for the crew.
- Android
  - Non-human crew members whose cold inhumanity unnerves those around them, even when they're trying to help.
- Scientist
  - Doctors, researchers, and specialists — deeply knowledgeable but prone to losing their grip on sanity.
- Teamster
  - Rough blue-collar workers — mechanics, miners, and pilots who are broadly capable but not specialists.
