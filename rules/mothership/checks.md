---
title: Making Checks
reference:
  - Player's Survival Guide, page 18
---

- Only make a check when there’s a significant risk, or a serious consequence to failure
- Determine which **stat** / **save** to check against
  - If you’re using one of your **skills**, add it to your **stat** for this check
- Roll a d100
  - Less than your stat: **success**
  - Equal to or over: **failure**, gain 1 **stress**
  - 90–99: always a **failure**
  - **Doubles**: critical (pass or fail)
    - 00 is always a **Critical Success**
    - 99 is always a **Critical Failure**
- **Advantage**: roll twice, take the lower result
- **Disadvantage**: roll twice, take the higher result
