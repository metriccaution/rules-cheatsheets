# Health

- If you do take a hit, damage is dealt to one of your character's **aspects**.
  - You choose which **aspect**.
  - If you mark all the boxes on a **track**, you can't use it until it's repaired.
- If you take enough damage to exceed a **track**, you choose one of:
  - Add an **injury** with a length equal to the exceeded value.
  - Deal **burn** to that aspect equal to the exceeded value.

## Weakness, Resistance, and Immunity

- **Weakness**: mark 2 extra boxes when taking damage from a source you're weak to.
- **Resistance**: mark 2 fewer boxes when taking damage from a source you're resistant to.
- **Immunity**: mark no boxes when taking damage from a source you're immune to.
- Stacking
  - 2 resistances = immunity.
  - Immunity + weakness = resistance.
  - Weakness + resistance cancel out.

# Dying

- Character death is **narrative**, not **mechanical**.
  - Fundamentally, you choose if/when your character dies, regardless of anything your character sheet says.
- There's enough weirdness in the setting that there's lots of ways death doesn't **have** to be the end.
