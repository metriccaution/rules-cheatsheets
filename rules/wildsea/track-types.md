---
title: Types of Track
reference:
  - Wildsea, page 38
  - Wildsea, page 39
---

- **Open** - The existence, progress, and length are visible to everyone
- **Hidden** - The existence of the track is known, but not progress or length.
  - The firefly may want to give in-game hints when you're close to marking the final box
- **Secret** - The players don't know about it; they only learn of it when they unwittingly mark the final box and the firefly reveals its secret
