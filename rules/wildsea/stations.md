Different roles on the ship. This doesn't cover everything - you can assume people are still busy even if they're not on-station.

- Required
  - **At the Helm**
    - Controlling the ship itself
  - **On Watch**
    - Make a watch roll to see what's happening around the ship
- Optional
  - **Tending the Engine**
    - Overcharge the engine by spending a resource as fuel
  - **Cartographizing**
    - Sketching out new charts of where you've been
  - **Watching the Weather**
    - Be forewarned of changes in the weather
