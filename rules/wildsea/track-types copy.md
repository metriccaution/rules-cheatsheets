- **Ardent** - Humans, but changed by the verdancy.
- **Ektus** - Cactus-people, long-lived.
- **Gau** - Fungus people.
- **Tzelicrae** - Hive-minded spider colonies.
- **Ironbound** - Haunted shipwrecks.
- **Ketra** - Human-descendants, changed by the deep-forest.
- **Mothryn** - Moth people that reincarnate.
- **Itzenko** - Rapidly evolving mantis people.
